Born and raised in Upstate New York. I am one of six children. As the youngest boy, I was taught to have a good work ethic, high attention to detail, and to be resourceful.
I played a variety of sports from youth into college. Working with teams developed my mindset that we are only as strong as our weakest link.
Supporting each member as well as putting in the effor to excel individualy leads to success. I carry that foundation with everything I do.

I will be forever grateful for the skills learned and life experience gained during that time of my life. Now, after a world altering pandemic,
I am looking to switch career paths in hopes of becoming a software developer. As I gain experience in this field, I intend to update these projects with more efficiency
and add new ones with the goal of making a difference in someones life. Thank you for taking the time to read this! I hope you enjoy my works.
